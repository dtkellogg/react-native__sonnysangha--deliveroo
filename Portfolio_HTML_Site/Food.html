Favorite Food

One of my favorite things in life is food. This can probably be said by most people, but I definitely embrace my love of food. Growing up with an amazing cook of a mother, I have been very lucky to be able to experience the greatness that is food from a very early age.

If you asked me to name my favorite food I would struggle. Burritos and sandwiches are a staple. Ramen and pho are necessities.
